### Privacy Focused
We believe your data is yours.
- **Browser Sync Storage**: All data is stored in your browser's sync storage and automatically syncs across all your devices when signed in with the same account.
  - **Edge to Edge**: Syncs across all devices where you're signed into Edge with your Microsoft account
  - **Chrome to Chrome**: Syncs across all devices where you're signed into Chrome with your Google account
  - **Not Cross-Browser**: Data does NOT sync between different browsers (e.g., Edge to Chrome)
- **No Servers**: We don't have a backend database. We don't see your data.
- **Exportable**: You can export your data to JSON at any time.
